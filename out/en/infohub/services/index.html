School Services

BG Zehnergasse uses several digital platforms for everyday school life. Here you will find an overview of all services and how to use them.

WebUntis — Timetable & Class Register

WebUntis is the digital timetable and electronic class register of the school, operated by Gruber & Petters (untis.at).

Access

DetailInformation
Direct linkmelpomene.webuntis.com/WebUntis/?school=bgzehnwn
Usernamelastname.firstname (not the email address)
PasswordYour personal school account password

Without login: The timetable, dashboard and office hours are publicly visible. Search for "BG Zehnergasse" on webuntis.com.

ℹ️Note for parents
Parents can access WebUntis through the Bildungsportal or use their child's login. For problems with your WebUntis parent account, please contact bnb@bgzwn.at.

Colour codes in the timetable

ColourMeaning
🟠 OrangeRegular lessons
🟡 YellowTests / exams
🟣 PurpleSubstitutions / changes

ℹ️Schedule changes
Changes for the next day are entered by 12:00 PM. After that, the displayed plan is final.

Reporting an absence

1

Click "Report absence" in the dashboard

Select the time period (full day or individual hours).
2

Select category "KR_neu"

Optionally add a comment for the class advisor.
3

Submit written excuse within one week

The absence is saved as "open" and must be excused within one week.
🚫Compulsory attendance
According to Austrian school law (SchUG § 43 and § 45), school attendance is compulsory. Parents must notify the class advisor by 7:30 AM on the first day of absence. A medical certificate is required for absences longer than one week or frequent short-term illness.

edu.Suite — Central Access Portal

edu.Suite by Quarto Software GmbH is the shared login portal for edu.Flow, edu.Pay and edu.Card.

DetailInformation
Portal URLedusuite.at/portal/
School code304046
Student loginVia Office 365 (Microsoft account)
Parent loginVia verified edu.Flow account
ℹ️Parent synchronisation
Parent accounts are synchronised nightly. Changes to your data will be visible the next day.
⚠️Personal data changed? (address, phone number, name etc.)
If your personal data changes (e.g. address, phone number, family name, guardianship), please send the update directly to your child's class advisor (Klassenvorstand). The class advisor updates the data in SOKRATES — from there it is automatically passed on to edu.Flow and edu.Pay. This is the only way to keep all systems up to date.

edu.Flow — Digital Parent Communication

edu.Flow is the official communication platform between school and parents.

Initial setup

1

Receive welcome letter

At the beginning of the school year you receive a letter with a QR code and access data.
2

Verify your email

Scan the QR code or enter the data at edusuite.at/portal/. Confirm your email address.
3

Return the signed letter

Give the signed letter back to the class advisor.
4

Start using the platform

With your email and password you now have access to edu.Flow and edu.Pay.
⚠️Emails not arriving?
Especially with hotmail.com, outlook.com, live.com and yahoo.com, emails are often blocked as spam. Add these senders to your contacts:
  • eduflow@bgzwn.at
  • edupay@bgzwn.at
Also check your spam/junk folder.

Forgot password (parents)

Reset your edu.Flow password here: Password reset. The IT team cannot reset parent passwords — only the self-service portal can.

edu.Pay — Cashless Payment Platform

Use edu.Pay to pay school expenses cashlessly:

  • Top up edu.Card balance (max. EUR 25 per top-up)
  • Order a new edu.Card (student ID, EUR 13)
  • Pay for sports weeks & excursions
  • Locker and bookshelf rental
Payment methodNote
EPS transferOnline bank transfer — credited immediately
Bank transferTakes 2–4 business days
ℹ️No card payments
Credit card and debit card payments are no longer offered due to high fees. Please use EPS or a regular bank transfer.
ℹ️Overpayment / Refund
Amounts under EUR 10 can be transferred to the copy card. From EUR 10 a refund is possible — send the application form with name, class and signature to kanzlei@bgzwn.at.

Portal Digitale Schule (PoDS)

PoDS is the platform of the Austrian Federal Ministry of Education for digital school administration.

Activation for students

1

Receive QR code in class

You will receive an activation code in DIGIB class. It is valid for 30 days.
2

Link with Office 365

Select "Register with Office 365" and sign in with lastname.firstname@bgzwn.at.
3

Confirm permissions

Confirm the requested permissions — your PoDS account is now linked to your school account.
